The Soul of Vietnamese Cuisine

What is Pho?

The key ingredients in traditional Vietnamese Pho include cinnamon and ginger, which help to warm the body, as well as onion and garlic, with other herbs added to balance the taste. Pho is especially tasty using the freshest ingredients to promote good health.

Rich & Hearty Soups

Taste the wholesome and hearty noodle soups we offer, available with vegetables, chicken, shrimp, steak, brisket, and more. We also offer vermicelli noodles and traditional Vietnamese rice plates.
